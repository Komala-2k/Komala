About Karnataka

History

Karnataka's rich history spans ancient dynasties, including the Chalukyas, Hoysalas, and Vijayanagara Empire. Their legacy lives on in the magnificent temples, monuments, and cultural traditions that define the state today.

Modern Karnataka

Today, Karnataka is a perfect blend of tradition and modernity. Bangalore, its capital, is India's tech hub, while cities like Mysore preserve the state's royal heritage. The state leads in education, technology, and sustainable development.

Natural Beauty

From the pristine beaches of the Konkan coast to the misty hills of Coorg, and the majestic waterfalls of Jog Falls, Karnataka's diverse landscapes offer breathtaking natural beauty and rich biodiversity.
